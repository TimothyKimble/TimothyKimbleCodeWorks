About

Tally

A full stack application utilizing Vuejs and Nodejs for creating game nights and hosting personal and party leaderboards. I had the opportunity to work with 4 fellow software developers while acting as scrum master over 2 weekly sprints. I spent a lot of time creating our UX/UI design utilizing figma and canva which was used in our live project. I also partner programed backend using Nodejs to establish object relationships and worked on front end using Vuejs.
